Bio

As a Research Assistant with experience at the Health Research Group, Department of Statistics, University of Rajshahi, I aim to advance evidence-based research in health psychology and behavioral sciences. My expertise includes survey data collection, statistical analysis using SPSS, NVivo, and Kobo Toolbox, and conducting systematic reviews and meta-analyses. I have authored multiple research articles and actively contribute to manuscript writing, data accuracy verification, and literature reviews. Passionate about utilizing quantitative methods to explore psychological disorders, mental well-being, and health-related behaviors, I seek to collaborate with leading experts to drive impactful research and policy development.
